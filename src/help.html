About VxCleaner

VxCleaner is a professional vocal restoration and enhancement plugin designed specifically for spoken voice. Its goal is to make dialogue feel clear, stable, and effortless to listen to without sounding processed.

VxCleaner is not a collection of independent effects. It is a guided system with a fixed signal flow, perceptual safety limits, and intentional constraints designed to preserve natural voice character.

Several corrective processes run automatically in the background. These are always active, carefully tuned for speech, and intentionally hidden to avoid unnecessary technical decisions.

Designed For

  • Voice-over, podcasting, broadcast, dialogue, and narration
  • Real-time use in DAWs and live monitoring workflows
  • Reducing noise, room coloration, and vocal inconsistency

Not Designed For

  • Singing or melodic material
  • Non-stationary noises such as clicks, bangs, keyboard hits, or other transient events that require manual or offline repair
  • Severely clipped or distorted recordings
  • Forensic spectral editing workflows

Modes

Simple Mode

Simple Mode offers three macro controls that adjust multiple processors at once. It is designed for fast, reliable results without technical decision-making.

  • Distance – Room reduction and proximity shaping
  • Clarity – Noise, de-essing, and spectral cleanup
  • Consistency – Loudness stability and dynamics
Advanced Mode

Advanced Mode exposes individual controls for precise influence over each processing stage. Some advanced controls indirectly influence automatic processes without exposing technical parameters.

Controls Explained

Clean & Repair
  • Noise Reduction – Reduces steady background noise (Normal/Aggressive)
  • Tone – Biases noise reduction toward rumble or hiss
  • De-Verb – Reduces room reflections
  • Clarity – Improves intelligibility
Noise Reduction

VxCleaner relies on the DSP denoiser described above, which combines adaptive spectral profiling, dynamic Wiener filtering, and speech-presence detection to transparently suppress broadband noise while preserving speech quality.

Our Advanced DSP Technology

VxCleaner's proprietary DSP engine represents the pinnacle of audio restoration technology:

  • Adaptive Noise Profiling – Continuously analyzes and adapts to changing noise characteristics
  • Spectral Masking Algorithms – Preserves vocal clarity while eliminating unwanted artifacts
  • Real-Time Wiener Filtering – Mathematically optimal noise reduction with minimal speech distortion
  • Voice-Optimized Processing – Specifically tuned for human speech patterns and frequencies
  • Zero-Latency Operation – Perfect for real-time monitoring and recording

Our DSP technology delivers professional-grade results that rival expensive standalone solutions, all while maintaining the natural character of your voice.

Polish & Enhance
  • Proximity – Restores close-mic body
  • De-Ess – Controls harsh sibilance
  • Leveler – Smooths loudness variation
  • Output Gain – Final level adjustment
Final Output Presets

Delivery contracts that enforce loudness and true-peak targets:

  • None - No loudness enforcement
  • Broadcast - -23 LUFS integrated loudness, -1.0 dBTP true peak ceiling
  • YouTube - -14 LUFS integrated loudness, -1.0 dBTP true peak ceiling
  • Spotify - -14 LUFS integrated loudness, -1.0 dBTP true peak ceiling
Automatic Background Processing
  • Speech High-Pass Filtering – Removes subsonic rumble
  • Plosive Softening – Controls low-frequency consonant bursts
  • Breath Management – Reduces breath prominence during pauses

These processes are conservative, speech-aware, and never mute the signal.
