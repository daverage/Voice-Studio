About VxCleaner

VxCleaner is a professional vocal restoration and enhancement plugin built for dialogue, voice-over, and broadcast work. The DSP chain is deterministic, real-time, and tuned specifically for speech so you can clean recordings without kybashing their tonal balance.

The plugin combines a fixed signal path with perceptual safety limits, automatic protection, and tight parameter smoothing so that you make musical edits without needing to become an audio engineer.

Simple Mode Macros

CLEAN

Boosts the cleanup stage. It raises Rumble, Hiss, the adaptive Noise Reduction gate, and the Static Noise capture so room tone is swept away first.

ENHANCE

Leans on the shaping stage ( Proximity + Clarity) to give the voice more body and articulation while remaining natural.

CONTROL

Commands the dynamics/damping stage. It raises De-Ess and Leveler activity and finally nudges Output Gain so loudness stays even.

The macros translate your fast, simple moves into precise multi-slider changes under the hood. You can mix simple (macro) mode with advanced pulls—every slider still responds when you switch tabs.

Factory Presets

Noise Removal Tool

Noise Reduction + Static Noise

The adaptive denoiser continuously analyzes the spectral noise floor, performs speech detection, and applies a hybrid Wiener-style filter that favors clarity over aggressive attenuation.

  • Noise Reduction is the primary slider—push it right to tighten the noise gate and bring down steady hum, air, and room noise.
  • Static Noise captures the steady room tone: hold Learn while the unwanted noise is playing, then release to let the filter lock onto that spectrum. Tap Clear to reset the learned profile if the noise changes.
  • The Quality meter below the noise controls reports how much noise is currently tracked. Stay around the middle of the meter to keep the noise floor suppressed without breathing artifacts.
  • All of this runs together: Noise Reduction, Static Noise, and the Learn/Clear buttons form the noise removal tool so you can handle both broadband and tonal stationary noise in one workflow.

Clean & Repair Sliders

Rumble

High-pass filtering focused on 20–120 Hz to tame mechanical thumps and stage vibration.

Hiss

Broadband attenuation above ~8 kHz that keeps air noise from creeping into the upper register.

Static Noise

Learns a constant hum/tone from the room; use the Learn button to capture noise and Clear to reset.

Noise Reduction

Adaptive noise floor reduction mixing spectral gating and energy-aware smoothing; dial in the amount of suppression.

De-Verb

Controls early reflection suppression to pull the source forward inside the mix.

Breath Control

Attenuates breaths and mouth noise between syllables while preserving the inhale energy.

Shape & Polish Sliders

Proximity

Restores low-frequency warmth for close-mic performances and compensates for distance.

Clarity

Sculpts the upper mids to remove muddiness and enhance articulation.

De-Ess

Limits harsh sibilance while retaining intelligible consonants.

Leveler

Smooths loudness variations with a transparent compressor- style curve.

Final Output

Gain

The last slider before the limiter; use it to match your DAW gain structure or to feed delivery presets.

Final Output Presets

Select delivery targets that enforce loudness/true peak ceilings: None, Broadcast (-23 LUFS / -1.0 dBTP), YouTube (-14 LUFS / -1.0 dBTP), or Spotify (-14 LUFS / -1.0 dBTP).

Technical Notes

  • All processing runs with deterministic, audio-thread safe DSP.
  • Parameters are smoothed to prevent artifacts whenever a slider jumps.
  • Hidden processes (plosive softening, breath management, etc.) always remain conservative to protect intelligibility.
  • The noise removal tool already combines adaptive spectral gating, quality monitoring, and a learned static profile.
