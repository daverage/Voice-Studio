Clear voice recordings, even when your space isn’t perfect.

VxCleaner is an audio restoration plugin built for everyday recording conditions. It removes background noise, room echo, plosives and harsh sibilance so voices stay clear, focused and natural.

No noise profiling. No over-processing. Just reliable cleanup that fits into modern content workflows.

What it handles

Background noise

Reduces hiss, hum and constant room noise without artifacts.

Room echo

Tames reflections so speech stays intelligible.

Plosives & breath

Softens thumps and breath clicks without dulling tone.

Dynamics safety

Built-in leveling and limiting to prevent clipping.

A simple, predictable workflow

VxCleaner is designed to be fast and dependable. Drop it on a vocal track, adjust a small set of controls, and get consistent results across very different recordings.

Insert on voice Reduce noise & room Shape breath & clarity Ready to mix or stream

Deterministic processing

VxCleaner uses traditional signal processing rather than opaque neural inference. That means low latency, predictable behavior, and no surprise artifacts mid-session.

Simple vs Advanced

Flip instantly between intention-based macros and the complete slider matrix. Both modes touch every stage of the deterministic DSP pipeline so you can stay fast or dive deep.

Simple Mode

Clean, Enhance, Control macros wrap the Clean & Repair, Shape & Polish, and dynamics stages into three intent-driven buttons.

Advanced Mode

Every slider and meter is exposed, including Rumble, Hiss, Static Noise, Noise Reduction, Proximity, Clarity, Leveler, and the Quality meter beneath the noise controls.
